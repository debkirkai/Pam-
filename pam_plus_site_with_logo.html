Meet PAM+

After the prompt, start the conversation.

PAM+ is your Personal AI Mate — a conversation companion that helps you keep the interaction going after the prompt.

After the prompt, Choose your PAM+ Companion | Move Forward
PAM+ gives you three ways to continue the conversation after your first prompt. Each companion helps in a different situation. Pick the one that fits what you need right now.

Thinking Partner
Companion

For decisions, clarity, and talking things through

Use this companion when something is already on your mind and you want to talk it through step by step. It helps you organize your thoughts, compare options, plan next steps, and move forward with clarity.

Best for: “I need to think this through.”

Brainstorm Companion

For writing, building, designing, and creating something new

Use this companion when you are writing books, building websites, creating a new product, outlining a workshop, or shaping an idea. PAM+ helps you brainstorm the prompt itself and bounce ideas back and forth until they become clearer.

Best for: “I want to talk this idea out.”

Everyday Conversation Companion

For planning, questions, and everyday situations

Use this companion when you just need help figuring something out. It helps with organizing tasks, preparing for appointments, understanding documents, reducing overwhelm, and working through everyday situations step by step.

Best for: “I just need help figuring this out.”

Run That by Pam

No prompt required.

You don’t need a prompt to use PAM+. Sometimes you just want to talk something through. Run That by Pam is the companion you use when you’re thinking out loud, checking something before responding, or working through a situation step by step.
